Vision

The Executive Command Center (ECC) is the strategic brain of The Home Land.

Unlike traditional dashboards that display historical information, the ECC continuously monitors operations, predicts future outcomes, identifies risks, recommends actions, and assists executives in making informed decisions.

The Executive Command Center is designed for:

* CEOs
* Property Owners
* Regional Directors
* Portfolio Managers
* CFOs
* COOs
* Investors

⸻

Philosophy

Traditional Dashboard

↓

Shows what happened.

Executive Command Center

↓

Explains why it happened.

Predicts what will happen.

Recommends what should happen.

Measures what happened after the decision.

Exemple :

Tu ouvres une propriété.

Le système ne montre pas seulement les informations.

Il dit :

Three strategic decisions are recommended today.

Puis :

1. Increase rent by 2.8%
    Impact estimé : +$48,000/an.
2. Replace HVAC this quarter
    Économie estimée : $17,000 sur 5 ans.
3. Renegotiate vendor contract
    Réduction estimée : 11 % des coûts de maintenance.

Chaque recommandation affiche :

* niveau de confiance,
* hypothèses utilisées,
* risques,
* ROI,
* délai de retour,
* possibilité de lancer directement un workflow d’approbation.
