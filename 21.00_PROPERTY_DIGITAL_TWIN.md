🏡 THE HOME LAND

Property Digital Twin

Version: 1.0

⸻

Vision

Every Property inside The Home Land has a Digital Twin.

A Digital Twin is a living digital representation of a real-world property.

It continuously reflects the operational, financial, technical, legal, environmental, and occupancy state of the asset.

The Digital Twin becomes the primary operational interface for managing a property.

⸻

Philosophy

Traditional software stores information.

The Home Land models reality.

Every Property behaves like a living system.

The Digital Twin continuously evolves as business events occur.
