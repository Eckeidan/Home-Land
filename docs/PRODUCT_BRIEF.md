# Product Brief

Status: Canonical  
Owner: Product  
Review cadence: Monthly during discovery

## Product statement

The Home Land is a multi-tenant property operations SaaS for US residential
property managers. It unifies portfolio structure, leases, rent operations,
maintenance, documents, and operational follow-up in one auditable workspace.

## Initial customer hypothesis

The first target is a professional property manager or management company with
approximately 100–5,000 residential units and an existing mix of spreadsheets,
email, accounting exports, and disconnected tools.

This hypothesis must be tested with at least 10 qualified interviews before the
team treats it as validated.

## Primary jobs to be done

1. Know what requires attention today across the portfolio.
2. Maintain an accurate relationship between property, unit, tenant, and lease.
3. Track rent obligations, collections, exceptions, and reconciliation.
4. Move a maintenance request from report to verified completion.
5. Give tenants and vendors a constrained self-service experience.
6. Preserve a reliable, searchable operational history.

## Product promise

Within five minutes, an authorized manager should understand:

- which payments are late or unreconciled;
- which maintenance requests are blocked or beyond SLA;
- which leases require action;
- which data or documents are missing;
- what changed since their last session.

## Differentiation

The first differentiation is an operational inbox, command palette, unified
property timeline, explainable health indicators, and assisted summaries. A 3D
Digital Twin is not required to prove the product promise.

## North-star and guardrail metrics

Candidate north-star metric:

`weekly organizations completing at least one core workflow`

Supporting metrics:

- median time to configure the first property and unit;
- percentage of rent obligations reconciled by due date;
- maintenance median time to first response and completion;
- weekly active managers and tenant self-service completion;
- number of manual follow-ups avoided;
- cross-tenant security incidents: target zero.

Final metric definitions require analytics contracts before implementation.

## Explicit non-goals for MVP

- Commercial, hospitality, university, government, and international workflows.
- General-purpose ERP or no-code platform.
- Automated tenant screening or housing eligibility decisions.
- Autonomous financial or legal actions.
- Marketplace, BIM, GIS, IoT, 3D simulation, and multi-cloud portability.
