Business Rules

1. Every entity belongs to exactly one Organization.
2. Every entity has a globally unique identifier (UUID).
3. Every entity includes CreatedAt, UpdatedAt, CreatedBy, UpdatedBy.
4. Every critical modification is audited.
5. Referential integrity must always be preserved.
6. Soft delete is the default strategy.
7. Financial records are immutable after posting.
8. Closed leases cannot be modified directly.
9. Deleted users never remove audit history.
10. AI never bypasses permission checks.
